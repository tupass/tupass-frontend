Why you should not enter real passwords

It is very important to keep passwords secret. So you should only enter your password on sites you created the password for in the first place.

Although we give our best to keep your input safe, it may be possible that third parties intercept your password.

 Local version

You are currently running the local version of TUPass. This means the whole application runs on your system without Internet access.

Please make sure that the address bar in your browser is pointing to localhost:8000.
